# connectai-biz

AI-native B2B outbound CRM for export and business development teams.

We are building MORI, a workflow-centered product that helps teams move from buyer data to action fast: contacts, brochures, signals, outreach, and follow-up in one system.

## What We Are Building

- Auth and onboarding foundation for multi-tenant workspaces
- Contact capture and card-scan based lead intake
- Brochure and asset workflows for outbound sales
- Compose, send, and signal-driven follow-up loops
- Settings, credits, billing, and operational controls

## Core Stack

- Next.js 16 + React 19
- NestJS 10 + Prisma
- PostgreSQL + Redis
- Docker-first local development
- ADR-driven planning and implementation

## Current Focus

- MORI MVP foundation
- Frontend and backend local development scaffolds
- Auth core, testing, and CI hardening

Most repositories are currently private while the product foundation is being finalized.
